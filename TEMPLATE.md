## Takeaway

State the claim or practice up front, in one or two sentences. A reader should know
what to do before they know why.

## When it applies

The conditions under which this holds — and, just as important, when it does *not*.
A tip without its applicability conditions is a trap.

## Why

The mechanism or reasoning. What makes this work.

## Example

Concrete, runnable artifact — a prompt, config, snippet, or command line. Real
artifacts carry the signal; prefer them over abstract description.

## Notes

Caveats, open questions, things still to verify. Link related notes with
`[[note-slug]]` wiki-links (slug = target filename without `.md`).
